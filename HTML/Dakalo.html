Background

Dakalo grew up in Thohoyando, Venda, and attended Vhembe High School where she developed a passion for computer science. As the first person in her family to attend university, she is determined to excel in her studies at the University of Pretoria. Dakalo is inspired by South African tech entrepreneurs who have built successful startups. She participates in the university's mentorship program, helping first-year students adjust to campus life.

Hobbies

Dakalo enjoys listening to music and enjoys drawing. During university holidays, Dakalo volunteers at a computer literacy program in her home community, teaching basic computing skills to school children and elderly community members.
