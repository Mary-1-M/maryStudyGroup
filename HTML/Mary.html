Background

Born and raised in Polokwane, Mary attended Taxila High School where she excelled in English and CAT. Her interest in technology began when she helped design a website for her school's matric dance. The positive feedback and the satisfaction of creating something useful inspired her to pursue studies in Information Technology. As a current student at the University of Pretoria, she's particularly interested in user experience design.

Hobbies

Mary has a passion for creativity, especially anything that involves drawing or crafting. On weekends, she enjoys participating in local running clubs at Menlyn. She's also a reader with a particular interest in graphic novels.
