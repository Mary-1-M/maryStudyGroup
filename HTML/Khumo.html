Background

Khumo comes from a small town near Nelspruit in Mpumalanga. She attended Lowveld High School where her Accounting studies teacher encouraged her to pursue a career in Accounting. She is the first in her family to study accounting and hopes to start a company after graduating.

Hobbies

Khumo is passionate about traditional Ndebele art and practices beadwork in her spare time, creating beautiful jewelry and decorative items. She enjoys cooking traditional South African dishes and often hosts dinner gatherings in her residence, sharing her culture with friends from diverse backgrounds.
